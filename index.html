OUR FOOTPRINT DNA

Marula Media is a Public Relations and Marketing Agency that was founded by two young black individuals in 2020. The founders of the agency believe that communication and marketing are two key drivers in ensuring that companies are well marketed and represented by making them relevant to their clients and stakeholders.

Public Relations and Marketing comes with a lot of passion and hard work within our organization. We believe that our clients deserve the best quality service that cannot be compromised.
